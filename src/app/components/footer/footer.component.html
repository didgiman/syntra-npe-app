Privacy Statement

1. Introduction

We highly value the privacy of our users. In this privacy statement, we explain what data we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it.

2. Collected Data

Our to-do app collects the following data:

  • Task data such as title, estimated duration, and sentiment score.
  • Data provided by the user for task predictions.
  • Interaction data with the "What's next" feature.

3. Use of Data

The collected data is used to:

  • Organize tasks and provide recommendations based on your input.
  • Generate motivational messages and tips via the OpenAI API.
  • Improve the user experience through personalized suggestions.

4. Data Sharing

We do not share personal data with third parties, except for the following purposes:

  • The OpenAI API receives task-related text (without personal identification) to generate motivational messages.
  • Technical data may be shared with our hosting provider for functional purposes.

5. Security

We take appropriate measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. Data is stored encrypted, and communication with our servers is secured.

6. Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as necessary for the use of the app. Upon request, users can have their data deleted.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to request access to your data, correct it, or have it deleted. Please contact us for such requests.

8. Changes

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be communicated within the app.

9. Contact

For questions about this privacy statement, you can contact us at info@nullpointerexceptions.be.
